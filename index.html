Delivering year-round professional mountain guiding and instruction in the West Highlands and beyond

Whether you'd like to learn how to move safely through technical terrain, want a guide to help you traverse the Skye Cuillin, or are stuck in a rut with your climbing grade and need some coaching, Phil Stevens Guiding can help you achieve your goals in the mountains.

I offer bespoke private guiding and instruction, with objectives and content tailored to meet your specific needs - after all, everyone's different. If you'd like to talk more about your aspirations or make a booking, please get in touch with me.
